About

Origin

Guaraná Antarctica is a Brazilian soda that was launched in Brazil on August 18, 1921, by the then Companhia Antarctica Paulista, under the name Guaraná Champagne Antarctica, becoming the first brand to market this type of soda.

Brazilian national team

In 2001, Ambev signed a contract with the Brazilian Football Confederation (CBF), securing Guaraná Antarctica as the official sponsor of the Brazilian national men's and women's football teams for a period of 18 years.

Guaraná on the Internet

In recent years, Guaraná Antarctica has used various internet tools and strategies to develop its brand advertising. Among them are social networking sites, games in the 'Alternate Reality Game' (ARG) format, and even a YouTube channel called 'Coisa Nossa,' which features the participation of well-known internet influencers such as YouTubers 'Matheus Canella,' 'Lucas Inutilismo,' 'Igor Guimarães,' 'Luba TV,' 'Maicon Küster,' 'Abelha,' 'Poladoful,' 'Doarda,' 'Laura Seraphim,' and 'Felca,' among other participants.
