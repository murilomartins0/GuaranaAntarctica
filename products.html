Guaraná Antarctica

The classic guarana beloved by all of Brazil! INGREDIENTS: Carbonated water, sugar, guarana extract, citric acid (acidulant), sodium benzoate and potassium sorbate (preservatives), flavoring, and caramel coloring.

Guaraná without sugar

The original flavor, now without sugars! INGREDIENTS: Carbonated water, guarana extract, flavoring, citric acid (acidulant), sweeteners: aspartame (34.8mg/100ml) and potassium acesulfame (9.0mg/100ml), sodium benzoate (preservative), and caramel coloring IV.

Guaraná Natu

Natu is made with 100% natural ingredients, without artificial preservatives or colorants, 0% sodium, and no added sugars or sweeteners. All of this without losing the original flavor of Guaraná Antarctica. INGREDIENTS: Carbonated water, clarified pear concentrate, clarified apple concentrate, clarified lemon concentrate, apple, carrot, and hibiscus concentrate, guarana extract, natural flavors, and steviol glycosides sweeteners (13.5 mg/100ml).
